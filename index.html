About

I'm Taylor Oliphant, a passionate and experienced full-stack developer based in Ohio. I specialize in building robust, scalable, and user-friendly web applications that help businesses thrive in the digital landscape.

With a deep understanding of both front-end and back-end technologies, I can handle every aspect of your web development project, from designing intuitive user interfaces to implementing complex server-side logic. My expertise includes:

  • Front-End Development (HTML, CSS, JavaScript, React)
  • Back-End Development (Node.js, Python, Databases)
  • Cloud Computing (AWS, Azure)
  • Responsive Design and Mobile Optimization
